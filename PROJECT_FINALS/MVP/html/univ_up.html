FACILITIES

UP Building

The University of the Philippines (UP) is renowned for its iconic campus buildings, each steeped in rich history and architectural significance. From the majestic Quezon Hall to the modern structures of its constituent colleges, UP's buildings embody the university's commitment to academic excellence and cultural heritage. These landmarks serve not only as centers of learning but also as symbols of UP's enduring legacy as the country's premier state university.

Library

UP's library system is one of the largest and most comprehensive in the Philippines, with each campus boasting its own extensive collection of books, journals, and research materials. From the Diliman Main Library to the specialized libraries in various colleges, UP's libraries provide students and faculty with access to a wealth of information across a wide range of disciplines. With modern facilities, digital resources, and knowledgeable staff, UP's libraries are vital hubs for academic research, intellectual inquiry, and lifelong learning.

Gym

UP's gymnasiums and sports facilities offer students opportunities for physical fitness, recreation, and competitive sports. From indoor courts for basketball and volleyball to outdoor tracks and fields for athletics, UP's gyms cater to a diverse range of sports and activities. Additionally, UP's gyms host intramural leagues, intercollegiate competitions, and community events, fostering a culture of sportsmanship, teamwork, and healthy living among students and faculty alike.
