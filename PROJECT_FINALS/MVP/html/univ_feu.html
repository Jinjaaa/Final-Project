FACILITIES

Far Eastern Building

Far Eastern University (FEU) boasts an architectural marvel that stands as a testament to its rich history and enduring legacy in the heart of Manila. The iconic Art Deco-inspired main building, with its majestic clock tower and intricate facade, captures the essence of the university's commitment to excellence in education and cultural heritage. Stepping into its halls, students are greeted by a blend of tradition and modernity, where historic architecture merges seamlessly with state-of-the-art facilities to create a vibrant learning environment.

Library

FEU's library serves as a sanctuary of knowledge and intellectual exploration, offering students and faculty access to a vast array of resources and research materials. With its extensive collection of books, journals, and digital archives, the library provides a wealth of information spanning various disciplines and areas of study. From quiet reading corners to collaborative workspaces and computer labs, it offers a conducive environment for scholarly pursuits and academic inquiry.

Gym

The gymnasium at Far Eastern University is a hub of athletic activity and physical fitness, where students can engage in a wide range of sports and recreational pursuits. Equipped with modern facilities, including basketball courts, indoor tracks, and fitness centers, the gym provides ample opportunities for students to stay active and healthy. Whether participating in intramural sports leagues, attending fitness classes, or simply enjoying a workout with friends, the gym fosters a sense of community and camaraderie among students.
