FACILITIES

San Beda Building

San Beda University's campus features a blend of historic charm and modern facilities, with its main building serving as a focal point of academic excellence. Steeped in rich heritage and architectural significance, the main building reflects the university's commitment to providing students with a conducive environment for learning and growth.

Library

San Beda University stands as a bastion of knowledge and intellectual inquiry, offering students access to a vast array of resources to support their academic pursuits. From books and journals to digital databases, the library provides a wealth of information across various disciplines, fostering a culture of research and lifelong learning.

Gym

San Beda University's gymnasium provides students with opportunities to prioritize their physical health and well-being. Equipped with modern exercise equipment and sports facilities, the gym encourages students to engage in physical activities, sports, and fitness programs, promoting a holistic approach to education and personal development.
