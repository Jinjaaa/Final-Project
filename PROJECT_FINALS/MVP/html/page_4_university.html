ADAMSON UNIVERSITY

Adamson University, nestled in the urban landscape of Manila, Philippines, is a prominent private educational institution known for its comprehensive engineering, science, and technology programs.

FACILITITES

Adamson Building

Adamson's Building stands as a testament to architectural ingenuity and historical significance. Situated in the heart of downtown, its imposing facade captivates passersby with its intricate neo-gothic detailing, a nod to the city's rich heritage. Originally erected in the late 19th century, it has witnessed the ebbs and flows of time, evolving from a bustling commercial hub to a cultural landmark. Its interior echoes with echoes of bygone eras, boasting soaring ceilings adorned with ornate moldings and grand staircases that whisper tales of the past.

Library

Adamson University's library, known as the Fr. Leandro Montañana, C.M. Library, is a central hub for research, study, and academic resources within the university campus. Located in Manila, Philippines, the library houses a vast collection of books, journals, periodicals, and multimedia materials covering various fields of study, including engineering, business, humanities, and science. It offers modern facilities such as computer labs, study areas, and group discussion rooms to cater to the needs of students and faculty members.

TOP COURSES

BS in Architecture

With the AdU's Bachelor of Science in Architecture program, the College of Architecture will not only prepare you to build new structures but also conserve old ones. By taking this five-year, ladderized program, you will be exposed to various opportunities that will develop your critical and creative skills which are both essential in its field of practice.

BS in Pharmacy

By taking their BS Pharmacy program, you will be trained in the effective delivery of pharmaceutical services. With their college facilities namely pharmacy laboratories, instrument room, animal laboratory, botanical garden, and pharmacy simulation room, you will surely gain knowledge and skills required in the profession.

BS in Biology

AdU's BS Biology program focuses on disciplines like molecular biology, microbial ecology, biodiversity, and ecology. The Department of Biology also collaborates with the Philippine Carabao Center (PCC) and Fisheries Biotechnology Center in Nueva Ecija, and AAMPHIL Natural Resources Exploration and Development Corporation in Dinagat Islands to provide students with hands-on experiences.

SCHOLARSHIP

Adamson University offers a range of scholarship opportunities to support its students. These include academic scholarships, which reward high academic achievers with either full or partial coverage of tuition fees based on their performance in entrance exams or academic standing. Additionally, the university provides athletic scholarships for talented student-athletes, covering various expenses related to their training and competition. Cultural scholarships are also available to students who excel in cultural activities, providing financial support to nurture their talents. Adamson University's scholarship programs aim to recognize and support students with diverse talents and backgrounds, ensuring that deserving individuals have access to quality education.
