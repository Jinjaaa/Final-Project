FACILITIES

Ateneo De Manila Building

Ateneo de Manila University's architectural landscape stands as a testament to its rich history and academic excellence. Its iconic main building, with its distinctive blue and white façade, commands attention as a beacon of learning and innovation. Stepping into its halls, one is greeted by a blend of tradition and modernity, where centuries-old wisdom converges with cutting-edge technology. From the grandeur of its chapel to the vibrant classrooms and research centers, every corner of the campus echoes with the vibrant energy of intellectual pursuit.

Library

The Ateneo de Manila University Library stands as a bastion of knowledge and intellectual growth within the university's vibrant academic landscape. With its modern architecture and serene ambiance, the library offers a welcoming haven for students, scholars, and researchers alike. Stepping through its doors, one is greeted by shelves adorned with a myriad of books, journals, and multimedia resources spanning various disciplines and topics.

Gym

The Ateneo de Manila University gymnasium embodies the spirit of holistic education, promoting not only physical wellness but also fostering a sense of community and camaraderie among its students and faculty. With its modern facilities and vibrant atmosphere, the gym serves as a hub for athletic development, recreational activities, and sports events. Equipped with state-of-the-art exercise machines, courts for basketball, volleyball, and other sports, as well as dedicated spaces for fitness classes and training sessions, the gym offers a diverse range of opportunities for individuals to stay active and pursue their fitness goals.
