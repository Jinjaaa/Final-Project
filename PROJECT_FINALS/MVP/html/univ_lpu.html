FACILITIES

Lyceum Building

Lyceum of the Philippines University (LPU) boasts a striking architectural presence, characterized by its modern design and innovative facilities. The university's main building stands as a beacon of academic excellence, with its sleek lines and contemporary aesthetic reflecting LPU's commitment to cutting-edge education. Within this architectural marvel, students find classrooms equipped with the latest technology, laboratories for hands-on learning, and collaborative spaces that foster creativity and critical thinking.

Library

LPU's library is a sanctuary for intellectual exploration and research, housing a vast collection of resources that span various disciplines and areas of interest. From print materials to digital archives, students have access to a wealth of information that supports their academic endeavors. The library's tranquil atmosphere, combined with modern amenities and study spaces, creates an ideal environment for focused learning and scholarly inquiry.

Gym

The gymnasium at Lyceum of the Philippines University is a hub of physical activity and wellness, providing students with opportunities to maintain their health and fitness while fostering a sense of camaraderie and teamwork. Equipped with state-of-the-art equipment and facilities, the gym offers a diverse range of activities, from individual workouts to team sports and group fitness classes. Whether students are honing their athletic skills, relieving stress through exercise, or simply enjoying recreational activities with friends, the gym promotes a balanced lifestyle and a positive campus culture.
