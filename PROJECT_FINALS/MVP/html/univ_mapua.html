FACILITIES

Mapúa Building

Mapúa University's architectural landscape is a testament to its legacy of engineering excellence and innovation. The university's main building stands as an iconic symbol of academic prowess, with its imposing structure and modern design dominating the skyline of Intramuros, Manila. Within its walls, students encounter cutting-edge laboratories, classrooms equipped with state-of-the-art technology, and collaborative spaces that foster interdisciplinary learning. The building not only serves as a hub for academic instruction but also embodies Mapúa's commitment to shaping future leaders in the fields of engineering, architecture, and technology.

Library

The Mapúa University library serves as a vital resource center for students and faculty, housing an extensive collection of books, journals, and digital resources that support research and academic inquiry. From engineering texts to architectural blueprints, the library's diverse holdings cater to the specialized needs of Mapúa's academic programs. With its modern facilities, quiet study areas, and group discussion rooms, the library provides an ideal environment for scholarly exploration and intellectual growth.

Gym

The gymnasium at Mapúa University is a hub of physical activity and athletic development, offering students opportunities to maintain their health and well-being while fostering a sense of community and sportsmanship. Equipped with state-of-the-art fitness equipment, sports courts, and multipurpose halls, the gym provides a variety of recreational options for students to engage in. Whether participating in intramural sports leagues, attending fitness classes, or simply working out with friends, students can pursue their fitness goals in a supportive and dynamic environment.
